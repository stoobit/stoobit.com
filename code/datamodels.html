Data Models

With the launch of Productivity Pro 2, we are excited to introduce a new file format called .pronote. Going forward, .pronote will be the preferred format for exporting and sharing Productivity Pro notes, while the .pro file format will be deprecated and primarily used for importing existing notes into Productivity Pro 2.
In an effort to facilitate seamless integration and support for Productivity Pro notes in other applications, we have made the Swift code for the .pronote data models publicly available. We want to emphasize that there are no licensing fees or similar obligations; all we kindly request is that you acknowledge the use of "Productivity Pro" in your implementation.

We genuinely appreciate and welcome everyone who chooses to integrate .pronote into their application, thereby contributing to the continued growth and accessibility of Productivity Pro.
For any inquiries or further information, feel free to reach out to us on X at @stoobitofficial.
